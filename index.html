Purpose

Retail is a rewarding business that involves making proposals directly to the end consumer, the customer .

Can we make this more exciting with the power of IT? That is the goal of SAZABY LEAGUE IRIS.

Business Contents

We work closely with business owners in the retail and food service industries, identifying business issues and working together to find solutions.

If you need to implement an IT solution, we will support you from planning and proposal, design and development to implementation and operation \
and maintenance,
working together to solve your problems and share in your joy.

Retail industry (especially specialty stores) knowledge

We provide IT support for a variety of retail businesses, mainly focusing on apparel, accessories, miscellaneous goods, cafes, restaurants, etc. We have a track record in handling projects that were launched in a short period of time and projects with many unique requirements.

The entire process from planning to operation and maintenance

We will propose the optimal solution after sorting out your requests and issues. We can support you throughout the entire system integration process, from planning and proposal to operation and maintenance.

Organizing teams to cater to different levels

We have teams organized to handle various levels, from the infrastructure layer such as networks, security and PCs, to the application layer such as POS, sales management and accounting systems, and our highly skilled staff are engaged in the work.

As IT experts specializing in the retail industry, we at IRIS will work together to help you make your business "more enjoyable," "more in-depth," and "more efficient." Rather than a vendor-user relationship, we will work as one team to achieve a common goal.
